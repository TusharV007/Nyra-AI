# Nyra — Digital Identity Guardian

**Nyra** is a privacy-first mobile application designed to act as your personal digital bodyguard. In an era where digital manipulation is becoming increasingly common, Nyra helps you discover, track, and report unauthorized use of your face and identity (such as deepfakes) across the internet.

## 🌟 What Does Nyra Do?

Nyra works in three simple steps to keep your digital identity safe:

### 1. Create Your Safe Profile

When you join Nyra, you create a secure account. Before Nyra can protect you, it needs to know who you are. You will upload a clear reference photo of your face, which acts as your "Secure Identity." Nyra locks your scanning capabilities until this is complete to ensure your privacy and security.

### 2. Run a Digital Scan

With your identity set up, you can tell Nyra to search the web for you. When you tap "Run Manual Scan," Nyra's powerful background engine goes to work. It searches the internet looking for mentions of your name and potentially manipulated images.

### 3. Review Your Evidence Locker

If Nyra finds anything suspicious, it doesn't just send you a notification—it locks down the evidence. Findings are instantly delivered to your "Evidence Locker" inside the app. Each piece of evidence is timestamped and secured with a unique digital fingerprint so that it can be used for reporting or legal purposes if necessary.

---

## 🛠 How It Works (Behind the Scenes)

While Nyra is incredibly simple to use on your phone, there is a lot of powerful technology working behind the scenes:

- **The Mobile App:** The app you interact with is designed to be sleek, minimalistic, and fast. It acts as your command center, letting you view alerts and manage your profile.
- **The "Private Investigator" Server:** When you run a scan, your phone talks to a secure background server. This server does the heavy lifting, currently using databases (like Wikipedia) to hunt for information rapidly without draining your phone's battery.
- **The Vault:** All of your profile data and evidence are stored in a highly secure cloud database (Firebase). Your evidence locker is strictly tied to your personal account—no one else can access your data. As soon as the server finds a threat, the Vault instantly pushes that alert to your phone screen in real-time.

## 🚀 The Second Phase of Nyra

Right now, Nyra uses advanced text searching to find potential threats as a working proof-of-concept. However, the vision for Nyra is much bigger:

### 1. Facial Recognition Internet Scans

Instead of just searching for your name, future versions of Nyra will take your Secure Identity Photo and perform a "reverse-image search" across the entire web. It will find exactly where your face appears online, even if your name is never mentioned.

### 2. True AI Deepfake Detection

When Nyra finds a photo or video of you, it will run that media through a specialized Artificial Intelligence detector. This AI will analyze the image pixel-by-pixel to determine if it has been mathematically altered or generated by AI (a deepfake), giving you an accurate "Threat Severity Score."

### 3. Automated Legal Takedowns

Finding a deepfake is only half the battle. In the future, the "Takedown" button in your Evidence Locker will automatically connect to the legal departments of major social networks. It will instantly generate and send formal legal notices on your behalf to get the fake content removed permanently.
